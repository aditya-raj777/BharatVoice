# BharatVoice – India's AI Benefits Agent

> Helping citizens discover government schemes they are actually eligible for.

BharatVoice is a simple AI-powered chatbot that helps Indian citizens find relevant government schemes based on their profile, eligibility, and needs. Users can interact in Hindi, Hinglish, or English and instantly get scheme details, required documents, and official application links.

---

## 🚀 Why BharatVoice?

Many government welfare schemes never reach the people who need them most because information is scattered across multiple websites and often difficult to understand.

As someone from Bihar, I have seen farmers, students, workers, and small business owners miss out on benefits simply because they were unaware of available schemes or didn't know where to apply.

BharatVoice aims to bridge this gap by making government scheme discovery simple, personalized, and accessible.

---

## ✨ Features

* 💬 Chat in Hindi, Hinglish, or English
* 🎯 Personalized scheme recommendations
* 📄 Document checklist for every scheme
* 🔗 Direct official application links
* ✅ Eligibility-based matching
* 🏛️ 120+ Central and State Government schemes
* ⚡ Fast and lightweight frontend application

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. User describes their situation naturally.
2. BharatVoice extracts important details such as:

   * Age
   * Occupation
   * Income
   * Gender
   * State
   * Category
3. The system matches the profile against available schemes.
4. Eligible schemes are displayed with:

   * Benefits
   * Eligibility criteria
   * Required documents
   * Official application links
